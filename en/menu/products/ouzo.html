Ouzo


Psychis Ouzo from Chios called “rempiko”. It took its name from the traditional bronze distiller, as it is called in the Chian dialect, its aroma from anise and care in distillation, the decoration of its bottle from the passion of the traditional craftsman of the island, when he creates, painting the walls with the style of “Sgraffito” and its personality from the elements of the heart of the Chian distiller. “To offer every person in the ends of the world the opportunity to enjoy the unique taste of Mastic and the traditional Ouzo of our island, Chios”
